About Us

Jogja is a special city with countless stories to tell. It’s a place that always finds a way to leave visitors longing to return. However, falling in love with Jogja feels incomplete without exploring every corner of its vibrant streets. Wondering where to go next in Jogja? MlakuMlaku is your solution! In Javanese, MlakuMlaku means "taking a walk," and true to its name, the app is designed to accompany your adventures around the city. More than just a travel guide, MlakuMlaku also showcases souvenirs available at the various tourist spots you visit.

Using MlakuMlaku comes with many benefits. One key feature is the ability to browse and purchase local souvenirs from specific destinations. This makes finding and shopping for authentic items easy while you explore. Another highlight is the review and rating system, where users can not only leave feedback but also read reviews from other travelers. This feature builds confidence when deciding which spots to visit, offering insights from real experiences.

The app also offers customizable itinerary templates, such as "Two Days in Jogja," which can be adjusted based on your preferences. You can add or remove destinations to create the perfect itinerary that suits your trip goals. Additionally, the app’s bookmark feature allows you to save favorite places to a personal list or even create themed playlists based on your interests. These playlists can be made public to share with friends or kept private for your own collection.

Lastly, #JurnalJogjaku is a platform within the app, inspired by Medium, where users can share their personal experiences in Jogja. Whether it's a memorable moment, travel tips, or a unique story, users are encouraged to write and share their adventures. It’s a space for travelers to connect, inspire, and relive the best moments of their journeys in Jogja.
